Li Qi
2024-04-18
手打transformer

前向传播

假设有一些向量的$m$维空间中，坐标点标准差会分别是$\mu(z)$和$\sigma(z)$，LayerNorm的参数是$w$和$b$，那么层归一化后的输出为：

$$
y = w \cdot \frac{z-\mu}{\sqrt{\sigma^2 + \epsilon}} + b
$$

这里$\epsilon$是为了防止分母为$0$。去掉这一项：

$$
y = w \cdot \frac{z-\mu}{\sigma} + b
$$

反向传播

假设损失函数$L$对输出$y$的偏导数是$\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y}$，那么这里需要三个梯度：$\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial w}$, $\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial b}$, $\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial x}$

令$\hat{x}=\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}$

两个参数的梯度非常简单：

$$\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial w_{i}} =\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_{i}} \cdot \frac{\partial y_{i}}{\partial w_{i}}=\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_{i}} \cdot \hat{x}_{i}$$

$$\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial b_{i}} =\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_{i}} \cdot \frac{\partial y_{i}}{\partial b_{i}}=\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_{i}}$$

对输入$x$的梯度等于：

$$
\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial x_i} == \frac{1}{\sigma} \cdot \left[ \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_i} \cdot w_i - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \left( \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j + \hat{x}_i \cdot \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j \cdot \hat{x}_j \right)\right]
$$

推导过程

对输入$x$的梯度可以写成：

$$
\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial x_i} = \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot \frac{\partial y_j}{\partial x_i}
$$

由于 $y = \frac{x - \mu}{\sigma}$, 我们可以写出 $\frac{\partial y_j}{\partial x_i}$ 的表达式：

$$
\frac{\partial y_j}{\partial x_i} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i} \left( \frac{x_j - \mu}{\sigma} \right)
= \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i} (x_j - \mu) \cdot \sigma^{-1} + (x_j - \mu) \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial x_i} (-\sigma^{-2})
$$

其中

$$
\frac{\partial \mu}{\partial x_i} = \frac{1}{m}
$$

$$
\frac{\partial \sigma}{\partial x_i} = \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-1} \cdot (x_i - \mu)
$$

代入可以得到：

$$
\frac{\partial y_j}{\partial x_i} = ( \delta_{ij} - \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial x_i} ) \cdot \sigma^{-1} - \sigma^{-2} \cdot (x_j - \mu) \cdot \frac{\partial \sigma}{\partial x_i}
$$

$$
= ( \delta_{ij} - \frac{1}{m} ) \cdot \sigma^{-1} - \sigma^{-2} \cdot (x_j - \mu) \cdot \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-1} \cdot (x_i - \mu)
$$

$$
= \sigma^{-1} \cdot \delta_{ij} - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-1} - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-3} \cdot (x_i - \mu) \cdot (x_j - \mu)
$$

$$
= \sigma^{-1} \cdot \delta_{ij} - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-1} \cdot 1 - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-1} \cdot \hat{x}_i \cdot \hat{x}_j
$$

最后可以得到：

$$
\frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial x_i} = \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j \cdot \frac{\partial y_j}{\partial x_i}
$$

$$
= \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j \cdot \left( \sigma^{-1} \cdot \delta_{ij} - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-1} - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sigma^{-1} \cdot \hat{x}_i \cdot \hat{x}_j \right)
$$

$$
= \frac{1}{\sigma} \cdot \left[ \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_i} \cdot w_i - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j - \frac{\hat{x}_i}{m} \cdot \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j \cdot \hat{x}_j \right]
$$

$$
= \frac{1}{\sigma} \cdot \left[ \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_i} \cdot w_i - \frac{1}{m} \cdot \left( \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j + \hat{x}_i \cdot \sum_j \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial y_j} \cdot w_j \cdot \hat{x}_j \right)\right]
$$
